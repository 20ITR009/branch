Benefits • Gently moisturizes your skin to keep it smooth • Mild Fragrance & Soft lather to pamper your skin • Dermatologist tested • Enriched with Glycerin & Natural Oils Pears transparent soap is a British brand of soap first produced and sold in 1807[3] by Andrew Pears, at a factory just off Oxford Street in London. It was the world's first mass-market translucent soap. Under the stewardship of advertising pioneer Thomas J. Barratt, A. & F. Pears initiated a number of innovations in sales and marketing. English actress and socialite Lillie Langtry was recruited to become the poster-girl for Pears in 1882, and in doing so she became the first celebrity to endorse a commercial product.
